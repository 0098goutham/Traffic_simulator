## 🔌 Connection Details

### LED Connections:
- Red LED → Digital Pin 8 → 220Ω Resistor → GND
- Yellow LED → Digital Pin 9 → 220Ω Resistor → GND
- Green LED → Digital Pin 10 → 220Ω Resistor → GND

### LCD (I2C) Connections:
- VCC → 5V
- GND → GND
- SDA → A4
- SCL → A5

### Power Distribution:
- Arduino 5V connected to breadboard positive rail
- Arduino GND connected to breadboard negative rail
